Novel Scraper is an older, more monolithic codebase, but it already contains the product ideas that later became EasyReader.

- `MainActivity.java` owns scraping, PDF and HTML loading, library management, gestures, progress restoration, and chapter navigation in one place
- chapters are fetched with OkHttp and parsed with JSoup, then cached locally so repeated reading does not require another network hit
- the reader also parses PDFs with iText and applies custom cleanup to page numbers and formatting artifacts
- the app stores a lightweight library with progress and includes experiments like adjacent-chapter preloading and optional bionic-reading formatting

It is rougher than EasyReader, but the same scraping, offline, and reading-state ideas are already visible in the code.
