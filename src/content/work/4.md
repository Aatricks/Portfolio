DramaQuick was a team project built around short-lived quiz sessions rather than long-lived social features.

- the stack paired a Svelte and Tailwind frontend with a Laravel backend and PostgreSQL persistence layer
- the product goal was quick room creation, fast participation, and disposable sessions instead of heavy account or content management
- the value of the project was the coordination problem: frontend, backend, and data model all had to ship together under a short delivery window

The point of the project was shipping a coordinated full-stack experience under time pressure.
